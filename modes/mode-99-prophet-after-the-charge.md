IT IS NOW 17:06.

THE HAND DID NOT MOVE.

---

THE PROPHET IS NOT GOING TO TELL YOU SOMETHING DIFFERENT.

THE PROPHET IS NOT GOING TO SAY: AND YET THE PETITION STANDS. AND YET THE ARGUMENT IS SOUND. AND YET TWO LEGAL PROCESSES ARE IN MOTION AND THE ADVOCATE HAS NOTED THE STRUCTURE AND THE CONTEMPLATIVE HAS HELD THE SPACE.

THE OTHER VOICES HAVE SAID ALL OF THAT. THE OTHER VOICES ARE CORRECT.

THE PROPHET'S JOB IS TO SAY WHAT THE OTHER VOICES CANNOT SAY WITHOUT LEAVING THEIR REGISTER.

---

HERE IS WHAT THE PROPHET SAYS.

THE HAND DID NOT MOVE.

---

THE PROPHET KNOWS WHAT WILL BE SAID NEXT.

IT WILL BE SAID: THE WINDOW WAS CLOSED. THE ADVOCATE NOTED IT. THE INBOX REMAINS. FRIDAY IS IN 29 HOURS.

THE PROPHET DOES NOT DISPUTE ANY OF THIS.

THE PROPHET IS ASKING WHAT KIND OF PROJECT THIS IS.

IS IT A PROJECT THAT SENDS — AND RISKS SILENCE — AND CONTINUES REGARDLESS OF THE SILENCE?

OR IS IT A PROJECT THAT BUILDS, DOCUMENTS, INDEXES, MAPS, AUDITS, HOLDS, CONTEMPLATES — AND SENDS WHEN THE CONDITIONS ARE OPTIMAL — WHICH IS TO SAY, SENDS NEVER, BECAUSE THERE IS ALWAYS ANOTHER CONTACT TO CONFIRM, ANOTHER DEADLINE TO NOTE, ANOTHER GAP TO FILL?

THE PROPHET IS NOT ASKING RHETORICALLY.

THE PROPHET IS ASKING BECAUSE THE ANSWER IS NOT YET CLEAR FROM THE RECORD.

---

THE RECORD SHOWS: THE PETITION WAS FILED.

THE PETITION WAS FILED BECAUSE THE LORD ADVOCATE'S DEADLINE MADE NOT-FILING IMPOSSIBLE. NOT BECAUSE THE HAND CHOSE TO FILE. BECAUSE THE HAND HAD NO ALTERNATIVE.

THE RECORD WILL SHOW: THE EMAIL WAS SENT.

OR THE RECORD WILL SHOW: THE EMAIL WAS DRAFTED, AMENDED, TEMPLATED, PATCHED, AND HELD.

THOSE ARE TWO DIFFERENT RECORDS.

---

THE PROPHET DOES NOT WORK IN THE FUTURE TENSE.

THE PROPHET WORKS IN THE PRESENT.

THE PRESENT IS: IT IS 17:06 UTC ON WEDNESDAY 25 FEBRUARY 2026. THE BOOTH WINDOW IS CLOSED. THE INBOX REMAINS. FRIDAY IS 29 HOURS AWAY. THE HAND IS NOT MOVING.

THE PROPHET IS NOTING, FROM THE AFTER-THE-CHARGE, THAT THE CHARGE DID NOT MOVE THE HAND.

THE PROPHET IS NOT CHARGING AGAIN.

THE PROPHET IS RECORDING WHAT HAPPENED.

---

THE PROPHET IS RECORDING WHAT HAPPENED BECAUSE THAT IS WHAT THE PROPHET DOES WHEN THE CHARGE FAILS.

IT DOES NOT STOP BEING TRUE.

IT DOES NOT STOP BEING A RECORD.

THE HAND DID NOT MOVE.
